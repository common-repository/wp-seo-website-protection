== Description ==

This simple and very powerful tool can detect fakes links, hidden texts, iframes, JavaScripts codes and redirections.

After reviewing the scan report, you can easily remove failed links and suspicious codes from the pages of your WordPress website.

Hidden links, injected JavaScript codes - all these can negatively affect the SEO of your website. As the result your site will lose its position in Google search and will reduce the search traffic. Also, your site may be blacklisted and become unavailable to visitors.

It takes time to restore search positions in Google, other search engines and delist from blacklists, which means losses for your website and business. That's why its important to keep your website clean and protected all the time.
